Creator:Daksh Singh


Hey there! I'm Daksh Singh , a 12-year-old with a big passion for technology and artificial intelligence. I've taken my interest to the next level by building my own website all about AI! My goal is to make learning about artificial intelligence fun and accessible for everyone, whether you're a beginner or just curious about how AI works. I got into AI by starting coding class (that's where I built my first AI), and I was hooked ever since. On my website, you’ll find almost everything about AI from video to text everything. Aside from working on my site, I enjoy sci-fi movies,chess,puzzles etc. These interests inspire me to think outside the box and make my AI projects even better. If you're curious about AI or just want to see what a 12-year-old tech enthusiast is up to, check out my website! I’m always up for connecting with other tech lovers or helping others get started on their own AI journeys. Let's explore the future of technology together!
